Community Rules

Please follow these guidelines to maintain a positive community

1 Use Common Sense
This includes being respectful to each other when interacting, as well as not doing (or abusing) things that are unintended. If you happen to find bugs, please create a ticket in the feedback-support forum on our Discord.
2 Don't Cheat
Seriously, don't. This is meant to be a fun experience for everyone, and this is only given if everyone adheres to the same rules. Therefore, any client modifications, injections, and anything else considered cheating is not allowed. You can take the definition of cheating as it exists on the official osu! servers and apply it 1:1 here.
3 Don't Create Multiple Accounts
As you are probably used from the official osu! servers, creating more than one account per person is a bannable offense.

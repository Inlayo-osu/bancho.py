How to Connect to Inlayo?

Get started in just a few simple steps

✨ Recommended Method - Inlayo Patcher

The easiest way to connect! Just download and run - that's it!

1

Download Patcher

Get the Inlayo Patcher to automatically configure your osu! client

2

Run the Patcher

Execute the downloaded patcher and it will automatically configure your osu! client

3

Start Playing!

Launch osu! and login with your {{ appName() }} account - you're all set!

Legacy Method (Manual -devserver configuration)

Follow these steps if you prefer manual configuration

1

Find osu! Shortcut

Find your shortcut to osu! on desktop and copy it

2

Edit Properties

1. Right click → Properties
2. Find "Target element" Box
3. Add " -devserver {{ domain() }}" at the end

3

Login to Account

Login to your {{ appName() }} osu! account and you should be done!
